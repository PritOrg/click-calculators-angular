Salary Breakdown Formulas

We use the following formulas to break down your annual salary:

Monthly Salary = Annual Salary / 12

Biweekly Salary = Annual Salary / (Weeks Worked / 2)

Weekly Salary = Annual Salary / Weeks Worked

Daily Salary = Weekly Salary / 5

Hourly Wage = Weekly Salary / Hours Worked Per Week
